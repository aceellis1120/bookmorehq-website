# AI Receptionist Closer Launch SOP

Last updated: June 10, 2026

## What The Closer Sells

BookMoreHQ installs a 24/7 AI receptionist that answers missed and after-hours
calls, qualifies leads, handles approved questions, routes urgent calls, alerts
the business, and captures appointment requests. Confirmed booking and customer
texting are included only when the required integration is active.

The closer is selling recovered revenue and faster response, not "AI."

## Sales Flow

1. Qualify the contractor.
2. Demonstrate the live phone number.
3. Select Starter, Growth, or Scale.
4. Open the package in the closer dashboard.
5. Copy and send the checkout link while still on the call.
6. Stay with the prospect until payment succeeds.
7. Have the client complete onboarding immediately.
8. Mark the deal paid and hand it to fulfillment.

Never accept credentials, API keys, card information, or sensitive client data
over text message. The client enters payment through Stripe and completes the
secure onboarding form.

## Qualification

A strong prospect has at least three of these:

- Missed calls or calls going to voicemail
- After-hours demand
- Average booked job worth more than the monthly fee
- Office staff overloaded with calls
- Slow lead response
- Multiple technicians, departments, or locations
- A calendar or CRM that can accept appointments

## Package Positioning

- Starter: one location, one number, straightforward intake and routing
- Growth: higher call volume, two flows or departments, CRM/calendar sync
- Scale: multiple numbers or locations, complex routing, custom reporting

Do not promise unlimited usage, immediate number porting, confirmed calendar
booking, SMS, a specific number of booked jobs, or an integration that has not
been approved by fulfillment.

## Core Demo

1. Ask the prospect to call the BookMoreHQ demo number.
2. Give them a realistic service scenario from their industry.
3. Show how the receptionist qualifies, captures urgency, and routes the call.
4. Show the resulting call summary.
5. Connect the demonstration to missed-call revenue.
6. Present one recommended package and send its checkout link.

## Common Objections

**"My customers want a real person."**

The system handles the calls that staff miss, cannot answer after hours, or
need routed immediately. It can transfer callers to a person when required.

**"AI will say the wrong thing."**

The receptionist is built from the company's approved services, policies,
hours, escalation rules, and prohibited actions. BookMoreHQ tests it before
launch and adjusts the configuration when the business changes.

**"It costs too much."**

Compare the monthly price with the value of missed calls, office coverage, and
one recovered job. Do not guarantee a return.

**"I need to think about it."**

Identify the unresolved concern. If the prospect is qualified but not ready,
schedule a specific follow-up rather than repeatedly pressuring them.

## Commission

- Starter: $1,000 after the client's payment clears
- Growth: $1,250 after the client's payment clears
- Scale: $1,500 after the client's payment clears

Refunds and chargebacks reverse the associated commission. Recurring monthly
revenue belongs to BookMoreHQ unless a separate written compensation plan says
otherwise.

## Realistic Activity Target

Four closed accounts in a week is a strong performance target, not a baseline
promise. A new closer should first prove:

- 80-120 targeted outbound attempts per day
- 8-15 real owner or decision-maker conversations per day
- 5-8 qualified demos per week
- 1-2 paid accounts per week

Once the list, offer, demo, and follow-up process are converting consistently,
the closer can work toward 3-4 weekly closes.

## Fulfillment Promise

Quote 1-2 business days for a standard launch after completed onboarding and
receipt of all required access. Custom integrations, SMS registration, number
porting, unusual routing, or delayed client approval can require 3-7 business
days or a separately agreed timeline.

## Handoff Checklist

- Payment completed through the assigned checkout link
- Client completed onboarding
- Package and closer attribution are correct
- Client understands setup and recurring charges
- Client understands included usage and overages
- Requested integrations are listed but not promised as active
- Fulfillment owner is notified of any unusual requirement
