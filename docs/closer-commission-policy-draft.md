# Closer Commission Policy Draft

Internal working draft. Obtain employment and contract counsel review before
signature or rollout.

## Package Commission

| Package | Commission |
| --- | ---: |
| Starter | $1,000 |
| Growth | $1,250 |
| Scale | $1,500 |

## When Commission Is Earned

Commission becomes eligible only when:

- Stripe shows the initial payment as paid
- The checkout is attributed to the closer
- The sale follows approved pricing and representations
- The client is not fraudulent or immediately disputed

The owner approves each commission in the dashboard before payout.

## Reversals

Refunds, chargebacks, fraudulent transactions, duplicate payments, or canceled
sales can reverse the associated unpaid commission. The final signed policy
must define how reversals are handled after a commission has already been paid.

## Recurring Revenue

Recurring monthly revenue belongs to BookMoreHQ unless a separate written
compensation agreement explicitly provides a recurring commission.

## Sales Conduct

Closers may not:

- Change approved prices or commission terms
- Guarantee revenue, appointments, or launch dates
- Promise unsupported integrations, SMS, or confirmed booking
- Collect card information outside Stripe
- Share dashboard credentials or checkout attribution links
- Offer refunds or contract exceptions without owner approval

## Records

Stripe payment records and BookMoreHQ dashboard attribution control commission
calculation. Any dispute should be raised promptly with the specific company,
checkout, and payment record.
