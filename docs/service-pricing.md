## Website Conversion

| Package | Price | Monthly | Closer Commission | Estimated BookMoreHQ Take |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Starter Website | $1,000 | $0 | $500 (50%) | About $200 |

| Premium Website | $4,000 | $0 | $1,400 (35%) | About $1,100 |

### Starter Website

Best for businesses that need a professional website live fast.

- Custom 1-3 page website
- Mobile-friendly design
- Contact form
- Click-to-call button
- Service section
- Google map embed if needed
- Basic local SEO setup
- Website launch/publish
- Google Business Profile website link support
- 3 months of minor website edits included

### Growth Website

Best for businesses that want a stronger online presence and more trust-building content.

Everything in Starter, plus:

- 4-6 pages
- Stronger service page structure
- Gallery section
- Testimonials section
- FAQ section
- Lead-focused layout improvements
- Stronger copy structure
- 6 months of minor website edits included

### Premium Website

Best for businesses that want a more polished, higher-converting website with long-term support.

Everything in Growth, plus:

- Up to 10 pages
- More custom design work
- Service area pages
- Advanced layout sections
- Stronger conversion-focused structure
- Priority turnaround
- Premium build quality
- 12 months of minor website edits included

### Minor Website Edits

Included:

- Text changes
- Phone, email, or address updates
- Swapping photos
- Updating hours
- Updating service lists
- Small section adjustments

Not included:

- Full redesign
- Brand overhaul
- Advanced custom features
- Major new page requests outside package scope

## Important Rules

- Do not activate checkout for a package until fulfillment capacity is confirmed.
- Do not promise ad results, booked jobs, or revenue guarantees.
- Ad spend is paid by the client directly.
- Custom integrations, advanced features, or out-of-scope rebuilds are quoted separately.
- Estimated BookMoreHQ take is before taxes, overhead, refunds, chargebacks, and owner distributions.
