# AI Receptionist Client Service Policy Draft

Internal working draft. Obtain Tennessee counsel review before using this as a
binding agreement.

## Payment

- The setup fee and first monthly charge are due before implementation begins.
- Monthly service renews automatically through Stripe until canceled under the
  final agreement.
- Usage beyond the package allowance may be billed at the published overage
  rate or require a package upgrade.
- Custom integrations, number porting, additional locations, and major rebuilds
  require separate written approval and pricing.

## Client Responsibilities

The client must provide accurate services, hours, service areas, escalation
rules, transfer numbers, approved answers, and required platform access. The
client is responsible for reviewing the call experience and reporting changes
to its business rules.

## Launch Timing

- Standard configuration target: 1-2 business days after complete onboarding.
- Integrations, SMS registration, number porting, complex routing, or missing
  access can extend launch to 3-7 business days or a separately agreed date.
- A system is not live until BookMoreHQ completes testing and marks it live.

## Service Boundaries

The receptionist does not diagnose technical problems, guarantee availability,
provide binding estimates, take payment, send messages, or confirm appointments
unless the relevant approved workflow is connected and completes successfully.

BookMoreHQ does not guarantee call volume, appointments, sales, revenue, or a
specific return on investment.

## Changes And Support

Routine wording and business-rule adjustments within the package are included
up to the package allowance. New departments, locations, integrations, major
call-flow changes, or redesigns can require additional fees.

## Cancellation And Refund Review

The final agreement should define:

- Required cancellation notice
- Whether monthly charges are refundable after a billing period starts
- Treatment of completed setup work
- Refund handling before and after provisioning
- Chargeback and unpaid-balance procedures
- Number ownership and porting responsibilities

No closer may promise a refund or cancellation exception outside the signed
agreement.

## Recording, Privacy, And Compliance

The final agreement must assign responsibility for legally required call
recording disclosures, consent, privacy notices, data retention, industry rules,
and use of customer information. Legal requirements vary by jurisdiction and
business type.
