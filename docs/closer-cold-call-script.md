# BookMoreHQ AI Receptionist Cold-Call Script

## Objective

Book a live demonstration or complete checkout with a qualified service-business
owner. The closer sells recovered calls, faster lead response, and dependable
coverage. Do not lead with technical AI terminology.

## Opener

> Hey, is this the owner?
>
> My name is [NAME] with BookMoreHQ. Quick reason for the call: we install a
> 24/7 AI receptionist for service businesses so missed and after-hours calls
> turn into qualified leads instead of voicemail. Are you the person who
> handles incoming calls?

## Discovery

Ask one question at a time:

1. What happens when your team cannot answer a call?
2. Do you receive many calls after hours or while technicians are working?
3. Who follows up with missed callers, and how quickly?
4. What is an average booked job worth to the business?
5. Would you want urgent calls transferred while routine calls are captured for
   follow-up?

## Transition To Demo

> That is exactly where this fits. Rather than asking you to imagine it, I can
> let you call the live receptionist now. It takes about two minutes.

Give the prospect the BookMoreHQ demo number and this scenario:

> My air conditioner is blowing warm air. I am in Nashville and would like
> someone tomorrow morning.

Ask the prospect to use their real name and callback number. After the call,
show the captured lead details and explain that the same alert would go to their
business.

## Package Presentation

Recommend one package rather than listing all three without context.

- Starter: $3,000 due today, then $1,000 per month. Includes 500 AI minutes.
- Growth: $4,000 due today, then $1,500 per month. Includes 1,000 AI minutes.
- Scale: $5,500 due today, then $2,500 per month. Includes 2,500 AI minutes.

The amount due today includes setup and the first month.

## Close

> Based on the call volume and coverage you described, I recommend [PACKAGE].
> We can start building as soon as payment and onboarding are complete. I will
> send the secure checkout link now and stay with you while you complete it.

## Common Objections

### “My customers want a person.”

> The receptionist handles calls your team misses or cannot answer after hours.
> It can still transfer urgent callers to a person based on your rules.

### “AI might say the wrong thing.”

> We build it from your approved services, hours, policies, escalation rules,
> and prohibited actions. We test it before launch and it does not invent
> pricing, diagnoses, or availability.

### “It costs too much.”

> What is one booked job worth? The comparison is the monthly fee versus the
> missed calls, slow follow-up, and office coverage you are already paying for.

### “I need to think about it.”

> That is fair. What specifically do you need to be comfortable deciding:
> the technology, the price, or how it would fit your current call process?

## Rules

- Never guarantee revenue, appointments, or a specific return.
- Never promise SMS, calendar confirmation, number porting, or an integration
  until fulfillment confirms it.
- Never collect card details. Send the assigned Stripe checkout link.
- Keep the prospect assigned to your personal booking and checkout links.
